BandWagon Discover local concerts

Description:

A music radio app that only streams music by bands that have an upcoming concert in your area. BandWagon offers a passive way to discover upcoming concerts you may be interesting in going to.

Features:

  • Works on desktop and mobile.
  • View information about the concert while you listen.
  • Offers links to buy tickets, add to calendar, and directions to the venue.
